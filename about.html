Who We Are

Novella was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to connect readers with their next favorite book. What started as a small online bookstore has grown into a thriving community of book lovers from around the world.

Our team consists of passionate readers and literary enthusiasts who are dedicated to providing exceptional service and carefully curated selections across all genres.

Our Values

  • Quality: We carefully select every book in our collection
  • Community: We foster a love of reading through events and discussions
  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly packaging and practices
  • Accessibility: We believe books should be available to everyone
